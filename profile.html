PROFILE

Who am i?

Hi, I’m Rose, I’m 17 years old, and I’m from Nootdorp. I’ve always had a passion for things that allow me to dive into different worlds and experiences. Video games are a huge part of that, especially Red Dead Redemption II and Subnautica. Both games are so well-crafted, with incredible attention to detail and a sense of adventure that I love. I can easily spend hours just exploring every corner of their worlds, getting lost in the storylines and the atmosphere. Outside of gaming, I’ve always been drawn to design, it’s something I find really exciting, whether it’s creating new concepts or playing around with different visual styles. It’s all about experimenting and finding new ways to express creativity.

I’m really into films that leave a lasting impression or make me think. I enjoy a variety of genres, from psychological thrillers like The Silence of the Lambs, which has such a gripping atmosphere and unforgettable characters, to sci-fi films like Annihilation, which blends beautiful visuals with deeper existential themes. I also enjoy horror films with a more unsettling vibe, like Speak No Evil (2022), which sticks with you long after the credits roll. And of course, I’ve always been a fan of Star Wars, it’s a classic that I appreciate for its storytelling and the vast, immersive universe it creates. Overall, I’m drawn to movies that offer something more than just entertainment, whether it’s a complex narrative, strong characters, or thought-provoking themes.
